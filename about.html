IIEST Shibpur

The Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur (IIEST Shibpur) is a premier technical institution located in Shibpur, Howrah, West Bengal, India. Established in 1856 as the Calcutta Civil Engineering College, it is one of the oldest engineering colleges in the country. Renamed as IIEST Shibpur in 2014, the institute has a rich history and has played a significant role in shaping the engineering landscape of India.
‍
IIEST Shibpur offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering and technology disciplines. Known for its academic excellence and research contributions, the institute has a strong faculty team and state-of-the-art infrastructure to support diverse fields of study.
‍
The campus is characterized by its blend of heritage structures and modern facilities, providing a conducive environment for learning and research. IIEST Shibpur has consistently been at the forefront of technological advancements, fostering innovation and nurturing bright minds to address the challenges of the ever-evolving engineering world.
‍
With a commitment to producing skilled and socially responsible engineers, IIEST Shibpur continues to be a hub for academic excellence, research, and technological innovation in the realm of engineering education in India.

Robodarshan

Robodarshan is the robotics and automation society at the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur (IIEST Shibpur). This student-driven organization focuses on fostering a passion for robotics, automation, and technology among its members. Robodarshan serves as a platform for students to explore and enhance their skills in these fields through various workshops, competitions, and collaborative projects.

The society plays a crucial role in organizing robotics events, hackathons, and technical sessions that provide a hands-on experience for students interested in the field. Robodarshan not only encourages theoretical learning but also emphasizes practical application, allowing members to work on real-world projects and gain valuable industry exposure.

The community-driven nature of Robodarshan fosters teamwork, creativity, and innovation, creating a dynamic learning environment for aspiring engineers. Through its initiatives, the society contributes to the overall academic and technical development of students at IIEST Shibpur, preparing them for challenges in the rapidly advancing world of robotics and automation.

Robodarshan embodies the spirit of technological exploration and serves as a testament to IIEST Shibpur's commitment to nurturing talent in cutting-edge fields, making it a prominent and integral part of the institute's vibrant technical ecosystem.
